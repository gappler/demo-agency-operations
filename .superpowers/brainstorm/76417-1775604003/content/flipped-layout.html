What changed vs. the first layout

Projects are rows
12 rows (retainers + projects) vs. 37 billable staff rows. Much more compact. Each row shows client health, budget info.
Team across the top
Column headers show name, role, and mini capacity bar. Filter by department tab to keep columns manageable.
Capacity footer row
Sticky bottom row shows each person's total allocated hours vs. target. Replaces the per-row capacity bars from Layout A.
Dept filter tabs
Toggle between departments to show relevant columns. "All Depts" shows everyone but scrolls horizontally.
